Welcome to Oracle Corporation

Oracle Corporation is a global leader in software, cloud infrastructure, and database technologies. We empower businesses across the world to innovate, transform, and thrive in a digital-first era. Our mission is to help people and businesses leverage the power of data to unlock opportunities and create new possibilities.

Explore our vast portfolio of products and solutions designed to meet the needs of industries across the globe.

Company Overview

Oracle was founded in 1977 and has since evolved into one of the world’s largest software and hardware systems providers. Our offerings range from cloud applications and platform services to enterprise databases and business analytics. Headquartered in Austin, Texas, Oracle has a global footprint with offices in more than 175 countries.

Our values are rooted in innovation, integrity, and a commitment to excellence. We believe in making the world a better place by fostering connections, improving security, and advancing technology for everyone.

Cloud Services

Oracle Cloud Infrastructure (OCI) provides secure, reliable, and scalable cloud solutions for businesses of all sizes. Our offerings include Infrastructure as a Service (IaaS), Platform as a Service (PaaS), and Software as a Service (SaaS), designed to support high-performance applications, data security, and advanced analytics.

Explore OCI to harness the power of the cloud and drive digital transformation for your business.

Enterprise Software

Oracle’s suite of enterprise software solutions includes database management, enterprise resource planning (ERP), customer relationship management (CRM), and human capital management (HCM) software.
